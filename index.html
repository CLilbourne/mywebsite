My Daily Learning Series

The Daily Series is a personal learning project where I commit to completing and sharing one project, experiment, or concept each day. The goal is simple, learn something new daily whether it's in computer science, data, AI, or something entirely unrelated and to make that learning public. Each post serves two purposes.

First, it’s a record of my continuous growth that I can go back to and use for future projects. Second, and more importantly, it's a resource for anyone reading. You might discover something new, pick up a helpful tip, or use my code as a springboard for your own work. Whether the project takes 30 minutes or several hours, the focus is on progress, curiosity, and sharing knowledge in a way that’s accessible and reusable.
